Welcome to Album Cover Downloader

This app allows you to download high-quality cover art for your saved Spotify albums.

How to Use

  1. Click the Login with Spotify button below to authorize the app.
  2. Once logged in, you'll see a list of your saved albums.
  3. Click Download next to an album to download its cover art.
  4. Use the Download All button to download all album covers as a ZIP file.

How to Disconnect

To disconnect your Spotify account and revoke access:

  1. Click the Disconnect button. After you have logged in.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated:2025-03-08

1. Information We Collect

When you log in with your Spotify account, we collect the following information:

  • Your Spotify account details (e.g., user ID).
  • Your saved albums and their cover art.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Fetch and display your saved albums.
  • Allow you to download album covers.

3. Data Storage

We do not permanently store any of your personal data. All data is temporarily stored in your session and deleted when you log out or disconnect your account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

5. Your Rights

You can disconnect your Spotify account at any time by clicking the Disconnect Account button. This will revoke our access to your data and delete all temporary data associated with your account.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication between your browser and our app is encrypted using HTTPS.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@albumcoverdownloader.com.
